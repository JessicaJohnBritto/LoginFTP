About Us

This is a service based company founded in 2020. We provide solutions to various corporate clients all over the globe in wide range of topics like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, AI Planning, Reinforcement Learning Web Development, Web Design. We are a group of highly trained professional from IIT Kharagpur who are enthusiastic about research in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning and willing to do corporate projects. These projects are acquired through the website and other short-term and long-term contracts. We also conduct independant research and already have a publication at the COLING 2020 conference and other ongoing papers. Our team currently consists of total of 7 members.
